Analog

There are many analog activities I enjoy that helps me wind down from a busy day working on computers.

I find playing the Ukulele relaxing. Hearing the rhythm slows things down. Taking hikes with my family and dog help refresh my mind. Working on my gardening puts things in perspective. Don't forget that cooking fresh veggies from the garden is good for you.
I have not gotten seriously sick since eating fresh vegetables!

Bicycling as a mode of transportation is a great way to stay fit.
I am also helping to improve the quality of California air.
